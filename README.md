# Project Overview

Customer retention is one of the most important business challenges faced by subscription-based companies. Acquiring a new customer is significantly more expensive than retaining an existing one, making customer churn a critical business metric.

In this project, I performed an end-to-end customer retention analysis using SQL, Python, and Power BI to identify the key drivers of customer churn and provide actionable business recommendations.

The project follows a complete analytics workflow—from data preparation and database design to exploratory data analysis, business intelligence dashboards, and strategic retention recommendations.

---

# Business Problem

The company has experienced a growing number of customers leaving its services but lacks visibility into:

- Which customers are most likely to churn
- Which products or services contribute to customer retention
- Which customer segments generate the highest revenue
- How customer support impacts churn
- What business actions should be taken to improve retention

The objective of this project is to transform raw customer data into meaningful business insights that help management reduce churn and improve customer lifetime value.

# Key Business Insights

The analysis revealed several important factors contributing to customer churn.

### Major Churn Drivers

- Month-to-month contracts exhibit significantly higher churn.
- Customers paying through electronic checks have higher churn rates.
- Customers with higher billing risk scores are more likely to leave.
- Low service adoption is associated with increased churn.
- Customers with multiple technical support tickets show higher churn probability.

---

# Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following strategies are recommended:

### Improve Customer Retention

- Encourage customers to switch to long-term contracts.
- Introduce loyalty rewards for long-term subscribers.
- Offer proactive retention campaigns for high-risk customers.

### Improve Customer Support

- Prioritize customers with repeated support requests.
- Reduce response time for technical issues.
- Monitor customer satisfaction after ticket resolution.

### Increase Customer Engagement

- Promote additional services to low-adoption customers.
- Launch personalized onboarding campaigns.
- Provide targeted product recommendations.

### Optimize Revenue

- Focus retention efforts on high-value customers.
- Monitor customers with declining engagement.
- Reduce revenue leakage caused by churn.
